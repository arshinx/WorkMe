Our Mission



Our mission is to remove the middleman in contracting small-scale handyman services. Homeowners and workers dictate the terms of transaction virtually, thereby removing any face to face social anxiety.



The Problems

Workers

Cleaning Companies and Major Employers often consume significant profits. Workers can work during extra hours for additional income.

Health

Social anxiety arises between homeowners and workers during monetary transactions especially when both parties are face to face.

HomeOwners

It is difficult to locate immediate services. It is expensive for a particular task or supported urgency of task.



The Solutions

Remove the middleman

User hires the handyman service directly. Homeowners and workers dictate the terms of transaction.

Reduce social anxiety

The terms of the labor are settled before the worker arrives at the user's home. There is no need for social interaction, thus removing further anxiety.

Instant results

Users have access to quick and instant service without the sacrifice of having to pay more for the completion of the task.
